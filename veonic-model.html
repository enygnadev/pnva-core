Veonic Layer

Veon

In PNVA-Core, a Veon is the minimal logical unit of causal influence activated by threshold. It is computational, not a physical particle claim.

Definition

Veon = minimal logical unit of causal influence activated by threshold.

Formal Sketch

S_t = { i | G_i(Phi_t) > theta_t }

nu_i,t = 1[G_i(Phi_t) > theta_t] * G_tilde_i(Phi_t)

D_i,t = alpha * grad(I_i) + beta * K_i(M_i) - gamma * A_i * u_i

V_i,t = nu_i,t * D_i,t

Phi_t+1 = Phi_t
        + eta_t * (sum G_tilde_j(Phi_t) + delta * sum V_i,t)
        + mu_t * (Phi_t - Phi_t-1)

Veonic Collapse

The moment a local possibility crosses threshold and enters the state update.

Veonic Density

The aggregate magnitude of active Veons in a step.

Boundary

PNVA does not claim that Veons create matter or prove new physics. The Veon is a logical computational unit.
